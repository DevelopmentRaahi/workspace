Overview

EDR solutions provide a comprehensive approach to securing endpoints—such as desktops, laptops, and servers—by continuously monitoring and analyzing activity on these devices. It helps organizations in identifying suspicious conduct, investigating possible threats, and taking prompt measures to minimize damage.

The Challenge

Traditional security tools like antivirus and firewalls often struggle with detecting new, sophisticated threats and lack real-time response capabilities.They depend on impact signature detection, which leaves a gap in protection.

The Solution

EDR solves this by offering continuous, real-time monitoring, advanced threat detection, and automated response. It identifies both known and unknown threats, allowing organizations to respond quickly and effectively, providing stronger protection against modern cyberattacks
